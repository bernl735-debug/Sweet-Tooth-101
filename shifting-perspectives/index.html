DIY "Fresas con Crema" at Home

Fresas con Crema, or "Strawberries and Cream" is a well-loved Mexican dessert that beautifully combines simplicity and flavor. While the dish draws inspiration from other culinary traditions, it has become a cherished part of Latin culture and identity.

Here is a quick guide on how to make them

Ingredients:

2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced

1 cup heavy cream

1/4 cup sweetened condensed milk

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

2 tablespoons sugar (optional)

Instructions:

  • Step 1: Cut the strawberries into small portions, and then proceed to add sugar.
  • Step 2: Mix condensed, heavy cream, vanilla extract and evaporated milk along with cream cheese in a blender.
  • Step 3: Mix both mixtures in a cup and enjoy!!
